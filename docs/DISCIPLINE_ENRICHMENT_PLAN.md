# Discipline Enrichment Plan — From Checkboxes to Experiences

**Date:** April 2, 2026
**Principle:** Don't just ask "did you do it?" — help them do it.

---

## Spiritual Capital

### Prayer
- **ACTS Framework** — Guided prompts: Adoration → Confession → Thanksgiving → Supplication. Tap through each one, write a sentence for each. Takes 3 minutes but gives structure.
- **Prayer List** — Ongoing list of requests with dates. Mark answered prayers. Optionally share with accountability partners.
- **Prayer Timer** — Silent prayer timer with optional ambient sound. "Pray for 5 minutes" with gentle chime.

### Bible Reading
- Link directly to today's reading from the Word tab
- "Tap to read today's chapter" instead of just a checkbox

### Worship
- Suggest a psalm or worship focus: "Today's worship: Psalm 100"
- Option to log what worship looked like (singing, listening, nature, etc.)

### Fasting
- Already connected to Fasting tracker (auto-checks when fast is active)

### Meditation on Scripture
- Pull today's verse from Word tab
- Guided Lectio Divina flow: Read → Reflect → Respond → Rest
- 5-minute guided meditation with scripture

---

## Relational Capital

### Serving Others
- Daily micro-action prompt: "Text someone and ask how they're doing" / "Help someone without being asked" / "Pay for the person behind you"
- Rotating suggestions so it doesn't feel repetitive

### Fellowship / Community
- "Who did you spend meaningful time with today?" — name input
- Builds a relationship map over time
- Encourages intentional community

### Encouraging Someone
- "Who can you encourage today?" with a text prompt they can copy and send
- Pre-written encouragement templates they can personalize
- "Send encouragement" button that opens their messaging app

### Family Time
- "What did you do with your family today?" — simple journal
- Suggested family activities by age group

---

## Physical Capital

### Exercise
- Duration tracker: "Log your movement today" with quick presets (15/30/45/60 min)
- Type selector: walk, run, gym, sport, yoga, etc.

### Rest / Sleep
- Bedtime check-in: "What time did you go to bed?"
- Track sleep patterns over time
- Gentle nudge if consistently late

### Healthy Eating
- Simple daily rating: "How intentional was your eating today?" 1-5
- Not calorie counting — awareness and gratitude

### Outdoor Time
- "Did you spend time outside today?"
- Connection to creation as worship

---

## Intellectual Capital

### Reading / Study
- "What are you reading?" — log book title + pages
- Build a reading list / book log over time
- Suggested reading list by capital

### Learning Something New
- "What did you learn today?" — one sentence journal
- Celebrate curiosity

### Creative Work
- "What did you create today?" — log creative output
- Photography, writing, cooking, building, etc.

### Planning / Strategy
- "What's one thing you're planning intentionally?" — brief note
- Connection to Proverbs wisdom on planning

---

## Financial Capital

### Giving / Tithing
- "Did you give generously today?" — yes/no with optional amount (private)
- Weekly/monthly giving tracker
- Celebrate generosity streaks

### Budgeting
- "Did you review your spending today?" — awareness check
- Link to budgeting resources

### Generosity
- Micro-generosity prompts: "Buy someone coffee" / "Leave a bigger tip" / "Donate to a cause"
- Not about amount — about posture

### Saving
- "Did you set aside anything for the future today?"
- Simple yes/no — building awareness, not a finance app

---

## Implementation Priority

1. **Prayer ACTS Framework** — highest impact, most people struggle here
2. **Lectio Divina for Meditation** — connects Word tab to discipline tracker
3. **Encouragement sender** — relational + low effort + high impact
4. **Exercise duration logger** — simple, tangible
5. **Reading/book log** — intellectual growth tracker
